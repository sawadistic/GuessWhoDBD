Guess Who

Dead by Daylight edition with every killer and survivor in the board.
Ask yes-or-no questions, eliminate suspects, then make the call.

How To Play: This game is meant for two players.
You both open the game and roll a hidden character.
Pick a question to ask your friend.
Click Yes or No based on your friend's answer.

Take turns asking questions to figure out each other's characters.
The game will automatically remove characters that do not match the answer.

Only answer Yes to Killer Questions for Killers and Survivor Questions for Survivors.
Subjective questions count as Yes and No so a character is not removed by mistake.

You get one Easy Question before Hard Mode.
You can skip the Easy Question by clicking Hard Mode.
Each question disappears after one use.

You can view Eliminated characters by clicking All/Eliminated.
You can bring back Eliminated characters by clicking Restore.
You can View, Undo and Redo your actions using the Last Action or Logs boxes.

Keep asking questions until you are ready to guess.

You can also ignore the questions and make your own then manually Eliminate instead!
